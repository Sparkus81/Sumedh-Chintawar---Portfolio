Formula SAE Pedal Box Heel Stop

HYTECH Electric Racing | CATIA V5, FEA | 2025
CATIA V5 FEA Structural Analysis 3D Printing Weight Optimization

Overview

Designed and fabricated a high-performance, load-bearing heel stop component for an electric Formula SAE race car's pedal box assembly. The project required achieving optimal structural integrity while minimizing weight and improving driver ergonomics under rigorous competition constraints.

3D printed model with heel shape testing
Pedal box setup with heel stop
V0 of Heel Stop
V1 of Heel Stop

Technical Approach

  • Structural Design: Engineered in CATIA V5 with iterative FEA validation to withstand 200+ lb pedal forces
  • Weight Optimization: Achieved 35% weight reduction through topology optimization
  • Material Selection: High-grade PLA carbon fiber hybrid for optimal strength-to-weight ratio
  • Analysis & Validation: Comprehensive FEA and physical load testing
Software CATIA V5, FEA Tools
Design Load 200+ lbs pedal force
Material High-grade PLA carbon fiber hybrid
Weight Reduction 35% vs. previous design
